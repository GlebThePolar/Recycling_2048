Who Has Your Data

During your usage of my game ("Recycling: 2048" app), some information about you is being collected. Generally speaking, this information is used for ads and some statistics. You can see more in the Privacy Policy.

All of this information is being collected and processed by Unity. I can access this data only through Unity, and for me, all of this data is already depersonalized. So, I can't even distinguish your personal data from data of other players. That is why you need to contact Unity directly - here, I will give you instructions.

Instructions on How to Request Your Data Deletion

As stated in https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy, users can request to delete their data by reaching out to DPO@unity3d.com.

Simply write an email to this address, and people from Unity will help you with your request. You can also find more information about Unity company here.
